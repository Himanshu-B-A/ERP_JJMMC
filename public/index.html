About JJMMC

A teaching hospital grounded in care, competence, character.

For six decades JJMMC, Davangere has trained doctors who serve communities across Karnataka and southern India. Our MBBS and postgraduate programmes follow the NMC CBME curriculum, delivered by senior faculty in a busy 700-bed teaching hospital attached to Bapuji Hospital & Chigateri District Hospital.

  • NMC-recognised MBBS and MD/MS degrees
  • AETCOM, self-directed learning & early clinical exposure
  • Skills & simulation lab, digital library, Wi-Fi campus
  • Modern OTs, ICUs, emergency medicine & imaging
